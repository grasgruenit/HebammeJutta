Aqua Fit for Pregnant Women

Feel light, supported and energized as your pregnancy progresses. In the soothing weightlessness of water, gentle movements keep you active without strain – ideal when your bump is growing heavier. The water acts like a natural lymphatic massage, easing swelling in hands and legs and leaving you refreshed.
Water is life – we all begin in it, and your baby is already floating in the protective warmth of amniotic fluid. In Aqua Fit, you’ll experience this natural element as a source of balance, vitality and flowing energy – for both you and your baby.
